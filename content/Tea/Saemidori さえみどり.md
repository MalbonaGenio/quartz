# Saemidori さえみどり

[[Kagoshima 鹿児島]] 1990, presents more [[Umami]], early shooting, good yield. 

Very promising cultivar, also good for [[Shaded teas 覆下栽培]].

Used [[Sencha 煎茶]], [[Gyokuro 玉露茶]], [[Tamaryokucha 玉緑茶]].
