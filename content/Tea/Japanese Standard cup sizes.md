# Japanese Standard cup sizes

[[Sencha 煎茶]] also know as sencha-wan, 80-120ml round shape white coloured porcelain.
[[Gyokuro 玉露茶]] also know as Gyokurocha-wan, ~30ml round shape white coloured porcelain.
