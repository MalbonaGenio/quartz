# Yuzamashi 湯冷し

Water cooler, used to help cool down water for high grade teas. Paired usually to a [[Shiboridashi 絞り出し]] or a [[Kyusu 急須]]. Can also be used as a "Justice cup" [[Gong dao bei justice cup]].
